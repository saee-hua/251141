How to style a scarf

  1. Pick your favourite scarf
  2. Place the scarf evenly around your head, shoulders, or neck depending on the style you prefer.
  3. If wearing as a headscarf, pin or tuck one side under your chin and wrap the other around your head.
  4. If wearing around the neck, cross one end over the other and pull it through the loop.
  5. Adjust the fabric so it feels secure and comfortable.
  6. Finish by arranging the ends neatly, or let them flow for a relaxed look.
